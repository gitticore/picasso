Looking for the best dentist in West Los Angeles?

Dr. Tamir Shams

Dr. Tamir Shams is an excellent and professional dentist dedicated in helping his patients achieve the smile they were meant to have. He received his graduate degree from Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. In addition to receiving early admission at Boston University, he was one of two students to be awarded the Merit Scholars Scholarship. Dr. Shams was the youngest to graduate from his dental program at 24 years of age.

A year after graduating from BU, he started his successful practice in Southern California in Cosmetic & Implant Dentistry. Creating new smiles has been Dr. Shams' passion.

In 2011 he decided to move his passion for creating beautiful smiles to El Paso. Although Dr. Shams is a diehard Los Angeles Lakers fan, he has grown very fond of the city of El Paso. He hopes to bring the quality of Beverly Hills dentistry along with the artistic Hollywood Smiles to El Paso.

Dr. Shams, along with Dr. Shayesteh, decided to bring Picasso Smiles back to the West Los Angeles area, with opening a new Culver City location in 2016.


Dr. Nima Shayesteh

Raised in Southern California, Dr. Nima Shayesteh ventured away to the East Coast to pursue his professional passion to attain his Doctorate in Medical Dentistry from Boston University's Goldman School of Dental Medicine. After successful completion, he returned to the Golden State to reunite with his family and friends and commence practicing dentistry.

A visit to the Sun City, he realized the local demand for El Paso TX dentists whose emphases focused on quality and esthetic dentistry. His artistic talent that had now crystallized during his practice in Beverly-Hills-type dental offices including his exposure to patients of refined taste, quality and expectations, made him the perfect candidate which El Paso lacked. As such, Dr. Shayesteh together with his colleague and classmate, Dr. Shams, introduced Beverly Hills dentistry to El Pasoans with Picasso Smiles Dental - Cosmetic & Implant Center.

The perspective and ethics he maintains on healthcare, supersede standard of care. In fact, Dr. Shayesteh has been attributed by his colleagues and co-workers for treating each one of his patients as one of his own family members. His commitment to patients indeed motivates him to strive for an exemplary level of attention, thoroughness, quality, and finesse in his dentistry.

Socially, Dr. Shayesteh is married and enjoys spending his out-of-the-office time with his wife & son, and their mutual families.

For the benefits of his patients, and his dedication to the pursuit of continuing education, Dr. Shayesteh continually updates his credentials and continuing education.
