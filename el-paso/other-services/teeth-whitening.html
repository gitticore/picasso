Teeth Whitening in El Paso
Zoom® Laser Teeth Whitening

According to America Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, tooth whitening, or “bleaching,” continues to be the most requested cosmetic dental service. Tooth whitening, under a dentist’s supervision, is about the safest cosmetic procedure you can receive.


If you’re looking for a whiter, brighter smile, then you’ve come to the right place! Dr. Nima Shayesteh and Dr. Tamir Shams are proud to offer quality teeth whitening to patients in and around the El Paso areas.

Zoom!® Laser Teeth Whitening

Zoom! Whitening lightens tooth enamel and dentin by using a bleaching process. This in-office procedure takes about ONE hour.


Steps
  • Hydrogen peroxide (the bleaching agent) is activated by the laser
  • The light breaks down the hydrogen peroxide
  • Oxygen enters your enamel and dentin
  • The oxygen bleaches any colored substances while leaving the rest of the tooth unchanged
  • Follow the Simple Post-Whitening Care Instructions

Proper Follow-Up Care
  • Daily Flossing
  • Brushing twice daily
  • Occasional touch ups (Zoom! Weekender or Nite White gel)

You may experience some sensitivity during the Zoom! Treatment. However, the laser light’s warmth should alleviate any discomfort. Ask your El Paso dentist for anti-sensitivity toothpaste prior to your teeth whitening treatment if you typically have sensitive gums.
